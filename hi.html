about

lyra messier—first name real, last name fake—makes dance music for the introverted. Inspired by progressive rock and metal bands like Porcupine Tree and Bring Me the Horizon just as much as the leftfield house of Nicolas Jaar and Bob Moses, her work is often lengthy and hypnotic, yet retains a staggering sense of melody. 2023 will see the release of an untitled fifth album on which she takes up vocal duties for the first time, though her mission remains unchanged: to make whatever sounds cool in her dimly lit bedroom, a process which tends to involve hooky beats and blaring guitar chords in equal measure.
